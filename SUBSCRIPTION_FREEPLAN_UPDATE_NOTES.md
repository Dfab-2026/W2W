# Subscription Free Plan Update

Changed only the subscription default/current-plan flow.

- New signup accounts now default to Free Plan for both Worker and Employer.
- Subscription page no longer shows Starter/Basic as the current plan for new users.
- Free Plan features are shown in the current plan card.
- Only Free Plan feature gates are enabled until a paid Razorpay subscription is activated.
- Paid Worker plans and Employer plans are unchanged.
- Worker and Employer profile UI/flows are untouched.
