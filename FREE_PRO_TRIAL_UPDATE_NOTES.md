Work2Wish Free Pro Trial Update

Changed only subscription plan display and new-user trial behavior:
- New worker/employer accounts receive Free Pro Trial access for 3 months.
- Subscription page does not mark Basic/Starter or the first paid plan as Current Plan by default.
- Trial is shown as Free Pro Trial, not as a paid plan.
- Paid plan cards remain selectable during trial.
- After trial expiry, app returns to Free plan and asks user to subscribe.
- Worker and employer profile/dashboard UI and other flows are unchanged.
