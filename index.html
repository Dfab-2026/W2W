Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Work2Wish, answered clearly and concisely.

What is Work2Wish?+

Work2Wish is India's blue-collar freelancing platform that connects skilled workers with local employers. It allows workers to earn extra income without leaving their current job, while helping employers find verified, skilled talent quickly.

Is Work2Wish free to join?+

Yes! Creating an account on Work2Wish is completely free for both employees and employers. We only charge a small platform fee on successful job completions.

Which cities does Work2Wish operate in?+

We currently operate primarily in Bengaluru with a strong presence in the Peenya Industrial Area. We are actively expanding to other major Indian cities.

How do I find jobs on Work2Wish?+

After creating your profile, our smart algorithm automatically matches you with relevant jobs near your location based on your skills and availability. You'll receive notifications and can also browse available postings.

Can I work on Work2Wish while keeping my full-time job?+

Absolutely — that's exactly what Work2Wish is designed for! You can take up jobs during weekends, holidays, or after your regular work hours. The platform lets you request jobs in advance so you can plan around your existing schedule.

How are workers verified on the platform?+

Workers go through an identity verification process during registration. Additionally, our rating and review system ensures that workers with a strong track record are highlighted to employers.

How do I post a job on Work2Wish?+

After creating your employer account, you can post a job by specifying the skill requirements, job duration, location, and budget. Our platform will then match you with suitable verified workers in your area.

How quickly can I find a worker?+

Most employers receive qualified candidate matches within a few hours of posting a job. For urgent requirements, our platform prioritizes your listing to ensure the fastest possible turnaround.

What is the Work Log feature?+

The Work Log is a real-time project tracking feature that allows employers to monitor the progress of an ongoing job. Workers update their progress and employers can review it anytime — ensuring full transparency.

How does payment work?+

Payments are processed securely through the Work2Wish platform upon job completion and employer approval. Workers receive payment directly to their registered bank account or UPI.

What payment methods are supported?+

We support UPI, Net Banking, and major debit/credit cards for employer payments. Worker payouts are made via UPI or direct bank transfer (NEFT/IMPS).

What is your refund policy?+

In cases of disputes or unsatisfactory work, Work2Wish has a structured dispute resolution process. Refunds are evaluated case by case in accordance with our Refund Policy. Contact info@work2wish.com for payment issues.

Still Have Questions?

Our support team is ready to help you — Mon to Sat, 10am–6pm.
